Tribute Page

A tribute page is a web page created to honor, celebrate, or remember a person, group, or event. It typically includes information about their life, achievements, and contributions, along with personal stories, images, and quotes. Tribute pages are often designed to showcase admiration and respect for the subject and serve as a lasting way to preserve their legacy.
